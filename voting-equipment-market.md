<p><p>The voting equipment market encompasses various systems for conducting elections efficiently. Optical Scan Paper Ballot Systems use printed ballots that voters mark and scan for tabulation. Direct Recording Electronic (DRE) Systems allow voters to make selections directly on electronic devices, often with touchscreens. Ballot-Marking Devices (BMDs) enable voters to mark their preferences electronically while producing a paper record for verification. Each type serves to enhance accessibility, accuracy, and security in the electoral process.</p></p>
