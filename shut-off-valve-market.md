<p><p>The shut-off valve market includes various types tailored for controlling fluid flow in piping systems. Ball valves, known for their quick operation and tight sealing, are ideal for on/off control. Butterfly valves, featuring a rotating disc, are lightweight and suited for large volumes. Gate valves excel in providing minimal resistance and are used for full-flow applications. Globe valves offer excellent throttling capabilities, making them suitable for regulating flow. Together, these valves cater to diverse industrial and residential needs.</p></p>
