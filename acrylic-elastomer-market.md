<p><p>The acrylic elastomer market consists mainly of two types: Acrylate Copolymer Rubber (ACM) and Acrylate Ethylene Copolymer (AEM). ACM is known for its excellent heat, oil, and ozone resistance, making it suitable for automotive seals and gaskets. AEM, on the other hand, offers enhanced flexibility and superior weather resistance, ideal for applications in electrical insulation and automotive components. Both types cater to diverse industries, providing tailored solutions for specific performance requirements in demanding environments.</p></p>
