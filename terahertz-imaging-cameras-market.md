<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Cameras market comprises two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, making it ideal for applications like security scanning and material characterization without external interference. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate the subject, enhancing image quality and enabling detailed analysis, which is beneficial for industrial inspections and biomedical applications. Both types cater to diverse needs across various sectors.</p></p>
