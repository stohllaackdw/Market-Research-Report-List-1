<p><p>The endovascular treatment devices market includes specialized technologies for managing aortic conditions. Endovascular Aortic Repair (EVAR) focuses on repairing abdominal aortic aneurysms using stent grafts, providing minimally invasive solutions. Conversely, Thoracic Endovascular Aortic/Aneurysm Repair (TEVAR) targets thoracic aortic aneurysms, employing similar stenting techniques to stabilize and reinforce the aorta. Both methods enhance patient outcomes by reducing recovery times and minimizing surgical complications, catering to growing demands for effective vascular therapies globally.</p></p>
