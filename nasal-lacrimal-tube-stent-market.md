<p><p>The nasal lacrimal tube stent market primarily consists of two types: mono canalicular stents and canalicular stents. Mono canalicular stents are designed for single-channel insertion, effectively maintaining the patency of the tear duct in cases of obstruction. In contrast, canalicular stents involve two channels, allowing for a more extensive drainage system. These stents are widely used in ophthalmology to treat conditions like dacryostenosis and improve tear drainage, enhancing patient comfort and eye health.</p></p>
