<p><p>The ionizing radiation sterilization market comprises three main types: gamma irradiation, X-ray irradiation, and electron irradiation. Gamma irradiation uses high-energy gamma rays to effectively sterilize medical devices and pharmaceuticals. X-ray irradiation employs high-energy X-rays for similar purposes, offering deep penetration with lower radiation doses. Electron irradiation utilizes high-energy electrons to sterilize products rapidly, often at lower costs. Each method provides unique advantages in terms of effectiveness, penetration depth, and efficiency, catering to diverse industry needs in sterilization processes.</p></p>
