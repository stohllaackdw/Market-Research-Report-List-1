<p><strong>The Echo type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>M Type</li><li>B Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Echo type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main types: A-Type, M-Type, and B-Type. A-Type ultrasound provides amplitude information, displaying a one-dimensional graph primarily used for ophthalmic examinations. M-Type ultrasound (motion mode) captures the motion of structures over time, often utilized in cardiology to visualize heart motion. B-Type ultrasound (brightness mode) generates two-dimensional images by varying pixel brightness based on echo intensity, widely used for comprehensive imaging in various medical fields including obstetrics and gynecology.</p></p>
