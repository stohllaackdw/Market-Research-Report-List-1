<p><p>The airport fence market comprises three main types: welded, bar, and woven. Welded fences feature rigid panels providing excellent security and durability, ideal for high-protection areas. Bar fences are characterized by horizontal and vertical bars, offering visibility while deterring intruders, making them suitable for various airport zones. Woven fences utilize interlaced wire strands, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in perimeter applications. Each type balances security, visibility, and budgetary needs, catering to the diverse requirements of airport facilities.</p></p>
