<p><p>The Dairy Quality Analyzer market includes ultrasonic and infrared technologies for assessing milk quality. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize sound waves to measure properties like fat, protein, and lactose content, offering real-time results and high accuracy. In contrast, infrared analyzers employ light wavelengths to determine composition and detect impurities, providing quick assessments with minimal sample preparation. Both technologies play a crucial role in ensuring dairy product safety, optimizing quality control processes, and meeting regulatory standards in the dairy industry.</p></p>
