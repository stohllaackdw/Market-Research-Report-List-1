Oxides, such as aluminum oxide and titanium dioxide, enhance thermal stability and mechanical strength in thermoset materials. Silicates, derived from minerals like clay and talc, improve flexibility and reduce brittleness. Hydroxides, including magnesium hydroxide, serve as flame retardants while providing structural integrity. Together, these fillers optimize the performance of thermoset polymers across industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction, meeting specific application requirements.</p></p>
