<p><p>The chemical resistant elastomer market is categorized into three main types: rubber, plastic, and silica gel. Rubber elastomers, known for their flexibility and durability, provide excellent resistance to a wide range of chemicals, making them suitable for seals and gaskets. Plastic elastomers offer superior chemical resistance with enhanced thermal stability, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Silica gel elastomers, characterized by their high resilience and non-toxic properties, are often used in food and pharmaceutical packaging due to their moisture-absorbing capabilities.</p></p>
