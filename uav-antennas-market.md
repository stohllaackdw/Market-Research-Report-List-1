<p><p>The UAV antennas market comprises primarily two types: portable and embedded antennas. Portable antennas are designed for mobility and ease of deployment, ideal for applications requiring quick setup in various terrains. These antennas often feature lightweight, compact designs to facilitate transport. Conversely, embedded antennas are integrated into the UAV's structure, optimizing space and enhancing aerodynamic efficiency. They provide streamlined performance and reliability for applications where consistent connectivity is crucial, such as surveillance and data transmission.</p></p>
