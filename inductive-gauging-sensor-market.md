<p><ul><li>NPN Inductive Gauging Sensor</li><li>PNP Inductive Gauging Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive gauging sensors are widely used in industrial applications for non-contact detection of metallic objects. The market is primarily segmented into NPN and PNP types based on their output configurations. NPN inductive sensors provide a sinking output, where the sensor completes the circuit to ground, making them suitable for controlling devices that require a negative signal. Conversely, PNP inductive sensors offer a sourcing output, supplying a positive signal, ideal for applications where a positive current is needed to activate devices. Both types cater to varying system requirements.</p></p>
