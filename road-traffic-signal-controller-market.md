<p><ul><li>Centralized Adaptive</li><li>Fixed Time</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road traffic signal controller market can be categorized into three main types: Centralized Adaptive, Fixed Time, and Others. </p><p>Centralized Adaptive controllers utilize real-time data to adjust signal timings dynamically, optimizing traffic flow based on current conditions. Fixed Time controllers operate on pre-set timing sequences regardless of traffic conditions, providing consistency but limited adaptability. The "Others" category includes hybrid systems or advanced technologies like vehicle-to-infrastructure communication, which enhance traffic management beyond traditional methods, offering innovative solutions for urban mobility challenges.</p></p>
