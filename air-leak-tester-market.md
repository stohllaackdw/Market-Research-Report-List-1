<p><p>The air leak tester market encompasses various technologies for detecting leaks in airtight systems. Differential pressure leak detectors measure changes in pressure to identify leaks, while flow leak detectors assess the flow of air to pinpoint leaks. Direct pressure leak detectors gauge the pressure directly within a system. Leak detectors for seals focus specifically on seal integrity, and helium mass spectrometer leak detectors utilize helium to find even minute leaks. Other leak detection methods may include ultrasonic or tracer gas techniques.</p></p>
