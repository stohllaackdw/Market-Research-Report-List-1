<p><ul><li>Full Flight Simulator</li><li>Flight Training Device</li><li>Computer Based Training</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Aerospace Simulation and Training Market can be divided into three main types. Full Flight Simulators (FFS) provide immersive, realistic flight experiences, replicating the cockpit and aircraft dynamics for pilot training. Flight Training Devices (FTD) focus on aircraft procedures and specific training tasks without the complete visual and motion capabilities of FFS. Computer-Based Training (CBT) offers interactive educational material, enhancing theoretical knowledge through modules and exercises, often used to support initial training and ongoing skill development.</p></p>
