<p><p>The at-home hair color and dye market includes three primary types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide a short-lived color, lasting through a few washes and ideal for experimenting with bold shades. Semi-permanent dyes offer a longer-lasting color, generally fading after several washes, suitable for those seeking commitment without long-term effects. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, providing lasting color that requires regrowth touch-ups, making them popular for full coverage and dramatic transformations.</p></p>
