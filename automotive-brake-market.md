<p><p>The automotive brake market consists of two main types: disc brakes and drum brakes. Disc brakes utilize a metal disc and calipers, offering superior performance and heat dissipation, making them popular in modern vehicles. They provide reliable stopping power, especially in high-speed applications. Conversely, drum brakes feature a cylindrical design with brake shoes that press against the drum's inner surface, often used in rear wheels of cost-effective vehicles. Both types serve essential functions in vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
