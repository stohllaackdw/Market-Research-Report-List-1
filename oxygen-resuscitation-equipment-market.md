<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment</li><li>Flow-inflating Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oxygen Resuscitation Equipment market includes two main types: self-inflating and flow-inflating devices. Self-inflating oxygen resuscitation equipment functions automatically, generating positive pressure with each compression, making it easy to use in emergencies. Flow-inflating devices, on the other hand, require a continuous gas supply and allow for controlled delivery of oxygen, offering greater flexibility in managing airflow. Both types are essential for providing immediate respiratory support in critical situations, catering to various medical needs in clinical and pre-hospital settings.</p></p>
