<p><strong>The Bipolar Electrostatic Chucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bipolar Electrostatic Chucks market comprises two main types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. The Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces generated by biased electrodes to hold thin wafers securely during processing. In contrast, the JR Type employs a continuous electric field with a specific voltage configuration, enhancing grip on the wafer by ensuring optimal contact and reduced particle contamination. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, providing precision handling and improved process efficiency.</p></p>
