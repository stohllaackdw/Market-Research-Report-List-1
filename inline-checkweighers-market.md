<p><p>Inline checkweighers are essential in quality control for manufacturing processes, categorized into two main types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously weigh products as they pass through the system on a conveyor, ensuring real-time monitoring and efficient processing. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers stop products for precise weighing, making them suitable for tasks requiring higher accuracy. Both types enhance production efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure compliance with weight regulations, ultimately contributing to better product quality and operational reliability.</p></p>
