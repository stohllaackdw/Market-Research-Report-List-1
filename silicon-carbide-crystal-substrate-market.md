<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) crystal substrate market encompasses three main types: conductive, semi-insulated, and insulating substrates. Conductive substrates facilitate electronic applications, offering low resistance for power devices. Semi-insulated substrates are designed to minimize parasitic capacitance, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Insulating substrates, while not highlighted here, typically provide electrical isolation. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing performance for various semiconductor devices, particularly in power electronics and high-temperature applications.</p></p>
