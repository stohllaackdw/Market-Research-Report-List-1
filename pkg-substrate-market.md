<p><p>The PKG Substrate market is divided into two main types: Total Ceramics PKG and Non-Total Ceramics PKG. Total Ceramics PKG refers to packaging solutions that utilize ceramic materials entirely for their substrate, known for their excellent durability and chemical resistance. In contrast, Non-Total Ceramics PKG incorporates a mix of materials, such as plastics or metals, alongside ceramics, offering flexibility in design and application. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics and food packaging, highlighting distinct properties and benefits.</p></p>
