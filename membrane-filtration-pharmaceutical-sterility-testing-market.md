<p><ul><li>Bioburden Testing</li><li>Sterility Testing</li><li>Bacterial Endotoxin Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane filtration pharmaceutical sterility testing market includes three primary types: Bioburden Testing, Sterility Testing, and Bacterial Endotoxin Testing. Bioburden Testing assesses the microbial load in a product, ensuring contamination levels are controlled before sterilization. Sterility Testing confirms the absence of viable microorganisms in the final product. Bacterial Endotoxin Testing quantifies endotoxins that may harm patients. Together, these tests ensure the safety and efficacy of pharmaceutical products, playing a crucial role in compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
