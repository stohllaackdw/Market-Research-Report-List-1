<p><p>The Ultra-Fast Handheld Spectrophotometer market encompasses several types of spectrometry technologies. U-V Visible Spectrometry analyzes light absorption and transmission in the ultraviolet-visible spectrum, while Raman Spectrometry measures molecular vibrations for material identification. Fluorescence Spectrometry detects emitted light from excited molecules, enabling sensitive analysis. Near Infrared Spectrometry assesses molecular overtones and combinations for various applications. Other types may include advanced techniques like terahertz or electronic spectrometry, catering to niche applications in fields such as pharmaceuticals, food safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
