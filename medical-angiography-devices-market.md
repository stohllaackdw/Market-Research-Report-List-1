<p><p>The medical angiography devices market comprises three primary types: X-Ray angiography devices utilize fluoroscopy to visualize blood vessels and assess conditions like blockages, often being the most common method. CT angiography devices employ computed tomography to provide detailed cross-sectional images of vascular structures, offering enhanced resolution and quicker diagnostics. MR angiography devices use magnetic resonance imaging, providing high-contrast images of blood vessels without radiation exposure, making them suitable for evaluating vascular conditions while ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
