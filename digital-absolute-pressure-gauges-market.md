<p><p>The Digital Absolute Pressure Gauges market is categorized into two main types: Fixed Probe and Connectable Probe. Fixed Probe gauges feature a built-in sensor that remains stationary, providing consistent measurements ideal for permanent installations. In contrast, Connectable Probe gauges allow for detachable sensors that can be easily connected to various pressure sources, offering flexibility for different applications and portability. Both types serve critical roles in industries requiring precise pressure measurement for monitoring and control processes.</p></p>
