<p><p>The Hydrogen Silicone Oil market is typically categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I is characterized by low viscosity and is primarily used in personal care products and skincare formulations, providing moisture and enhancing texture. Type II, on the other hand, has higher viscosity and is utilized in industrial applications, such as lubricants and coatings, offering superior thermal stability and at times, enhanced water repellency. Both types cater to distinct needs across various industries, driving market demand.</p></p>
