The market can be categorized into two main types: low resistance testing and large resistance testing. Low resistance testing focuses on measuring very small resistances, crucial for applications like detecting connections in electrical circuits. Both types are essential for ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical systems and devices.</p></p>
