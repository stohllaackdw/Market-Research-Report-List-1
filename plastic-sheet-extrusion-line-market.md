<p><p>The plastic sheet extrusion line market is primarily categorized into two types: multilayer and single layer. Single-layer extrusion involves producing sheets from a single type of resin, resulting in simpler structures suitable for basic applications. In contrast, multilayer extrusion combines different materials in layers, enhancing properties like strength, barrier resistance, and aesthetics. This versatility allows multilayer sheets to cater to diverse industries, including packaging, construction, and automotive, offering tailored solutions for specific performance requirements.</p></p>
