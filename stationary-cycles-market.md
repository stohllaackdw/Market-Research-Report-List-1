Magnetic stationary cycles utilize magnets to create resistance, offering a smooth ride and adjustable tension. Electromagnetic cycles employ electromagnetic systems for precise resistance control, allowing for a varied workout experience. Self-generating stationary cycles harness the rider's energy to power the display and features, making them eco-friendly and convenient. Each type caters to different fitness preferences, enhancing the experience for users seeking effective workouts.</p></p>
