<p><p>The Patient Portal market is divided into two main types: standalone and integrated patient portals. Standalone patient portals operate independently from electronic health record (EHR) systems, providing patients with features like appointment scheduling, test results access, and communication tools. In contrast, integrated patient portals are embedded within EHR systems, offering seamless access to comprehensive patient data and clinical workflows. This integration enhances the patient experience by allowing for more cohesive interactions with healthcare providers and better management of health information.</p></p>
