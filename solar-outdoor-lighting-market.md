<p><p>The Solar Outdoor Lighting market is divided into two main types: Off-Grid and On-Grid systems. Off-Grid solar lighting operates independently from the electricity grid, utilizing solar panels and batteries for energy, making it ideal for remote areas without access to conventional power. In contrast, On-Grid solar lighting connects to the electricity grid, allowing users to draw power from both solar energy and the grid, often enabling cost savings and reducing reliance on conventional energy sources.</p></p>
