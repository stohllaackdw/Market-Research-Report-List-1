<p><p>Class II Biosafety Cabinets are specialized equipment designed to protect users, samples, and the environment from biohazardous materials. They are categorized into three types: </p><p>- **Type A** cabinets recirculate air within the unit and exhaust a portion, suitable for handling low-risk materials. </p><p>- **Type B** cabinets exhaust air outside the lab, ideal for volatile chemicals and more hazardous pathogens. </p><p>- **Type C** combines features of both, offering flexibility in airflow management and is often used in advanced research settings. Each type addresses specific safety and operational needs in laboratory environments.</p></p>
