<p><p>Low Temperature Effusion Cells (LHEZ) are crucial for thin film deposition in various applications, such as semiconductor processing and materials science. The market is segmented into two primary types: Single Filament LHEZ and Dual Filament LHEZ. Single Filament LHEZ utilizes one filament for material evaporation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Dual Filament LHEZ employs two filaments, allowing for more precise control over deposition rates and materials, enhancing performance, and flexibility in complex layering processes.</p></p>
