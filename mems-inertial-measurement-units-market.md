<p><strong>The MEMS Inertial Measurement Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>4DoF</li><li>6DoF</li><li>9DoF</li><li>10DoF</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs) are categorized by their degrees of freedom (DoF). 4DoF typically includes basic accelerometers and gyroscopes for movement detection in two dimensions. 6DoF combines 3 accelerometers and 3 gyroscopes, enabling full 3D motion tracking. 9DoF adds magnetometers for orientation in a magnetic field. 10DoF further enhances performance with additional sensors like pressure or temperature sensors. Other markets may include specialized IMUs designed for niche applications, integrating varying sensor types based on industry needs.</p></p>
