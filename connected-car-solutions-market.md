<p><p>Connected car solutions can be divided into several types based on the communication between vehicles and their surroundings. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication enables cars to exchange information about traffic conditions, hazards, and routing directly with each other, enhancing safety and efficiency. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) involves communication between vehicles and road infrastructure, such as traffic lights and signage, optimizing traffic management. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) technology helps vehicles detect and communicate with pedestrians to improve safety, reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
