<p><p>The hygienic control valves market is categorized into two primary types based on actuation: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators provide straight-line motion to the valve, making them ideal for applications requiring precise flow control, such as in pharmaceutical and food processing industries. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, utilize rotational motion for valve operation, suitable for high-torque applications. Both types are designed to maintain strict hygiene standards and are essential for ensuring product purity and system efficiency.</p></p>
