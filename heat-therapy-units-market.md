<p><p>The heat therapy units market encompasses various technologies that deliver therapeutic heat for pain relief and tissue healing. Ultrasound diathermy units utilize sound waves to generate heat deep within tissues, enhancing circulation. Microwave diathermy units employ microwave radiation for effective heating of larger areas. Shortwave diathermy units use high-frequency electromagnetic waves for deep tissue treatment. Infrared diathermy units provide heat through infrared light, targeting superficial tissues. Other market options may include novel modalities and hybrid systems that combine these approaches for improved therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
