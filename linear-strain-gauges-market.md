<p><ul><li>Resistive Strain Gauge</li><li>Fiber Optic Strain Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear strain gauge market comprises primarily resistive strain gauges and fiber optic strain gauges. Resistive strain gauges measure deformation by detecting changes in electrical resistance caused by tensile or compressive forces. They are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and simplicity. In contrast, fiber optic strain gauges utilize optical fibers to detect strain through changes in light properties. They excel in harsh environments and offer high sensitivity, making them ideal for applications requiring precise measurements over long distances.</p></p>
